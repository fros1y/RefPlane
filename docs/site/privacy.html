Privacy Policy

Effective April 4, 2026 · Last updated April 4, 2026

Overview

Underpaint is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app collects no personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Underpaint collects no personal data.

The app uses no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts.

Image Processing

Images you choose are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. No images, thumbnails, or metadata leave your device at any time.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library only so you can choose reference images. Underpaint reads the selected image; it does not scan, index, or access other photos in your library.

Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. Underpaint does not collect, store, or transmit any payment information. Purchase history and entitlements are managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework on your device.

Third-Party Services

Underpaint uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporters, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Underpaint does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app collects no information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact mgalese@icloud.com.
