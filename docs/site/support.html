Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do for free?

The free version of Underpaint lets you explore all four study modes (Original, Tonal, Value, and Color) using the six built-in sample images. You can adjust settings, view paint recipes, and export results — everything works on samples.

How do I unlock the full app?

Tap the Library button (it shows a lock icon) or go to About > Unlock Full App. A one-time purchase unlocks the ability to load any photo from your library. The unlock is permanent and supports Family Sharing.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the About screen (tap the ⓘ icon in the toolbar) and tap "Restore Purchases." This will check your Apple ID for an existing purchase and re-activate the full app if one is found.

How do I redeem a promo code?

Open the About screen and tap "Redeem Code." This opens Apple's standard code redemption sheet where you can enter your code. Alternatively, you can redeem codes through the App Store app.

Does Underpaint work offline?

Yes. All image processing happens entirely on your device. No internet connection is required to use the app after installation. A connection is only needed for the initial purchase or restore.

Does the purchase include Family Sharing?

Yes. When you purchase Underpaint, everyone in your Family Sharing group gets access at no additional cost.

What devices are supported?

Underpaint runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 18.0 or later.
